Adds the odd watch, an item craftable for free anywhere from the "Other" tab.

While worn -and- activated, grants instant crafting, construction and speed. Deactivate to revert the effects.

Due to how speed works, you will need to manually wait a turn after deactivating the watch for your speed to return.

Quick bandaid for low-end/mobile players togglable with minimal inputs, for use in laggy areas and such. Use responsibly.
